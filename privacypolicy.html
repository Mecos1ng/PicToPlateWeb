Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

1. Scope

This policy covers what data is collected and how it is managed during the use of the PicToPlateAI mobile application and related services.

2. Age and Parental Information

Users over the age of 7 are requested to use our application. Users under 18 are encouraged to use the app under parental supervision. Protecting children's privacy is our priority.

3. Categories of Data We Process

We collect the following data during the onboarding process and during use:

  • Profile Information: Gender, date of birth (age), height, and weight.
  • Nutrition and Lifestyle: Diet type, weekly exercise frequency, history of calorie tracker app use.
  • Marketing Data: Where you heard about us and referral/discount codes.
  • Visual Data: Food photos uploaded for analysis.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

We process collected data for the following primary purposes:

  • To provide a personalized experience during onboarding.
  • To calculate calories and macros (protein, fat, carbs) via AI.
  • To create nutrition advice and goals based on your profile.
  • To manage in-app subscription discounts and campaigns.

5. Data Sharing (Data Processors)

We only share data with trusted partners to maintain our service quality:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication.
  • Google Play Services & App Store: For subscription transactions and app performance tracking.

6. International Data Transfer

Cloud services we use (like Supabase) may host your data on secure servers outside of Turkey. These transfers are subject to standard protection protocols.

7. Retention and Deletion

We store your data as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the service. When you delete your account, all data not legally required to be kept is permanently cleaned from our systems.

8. Security

We implement technical and administrative security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

9. Your Choices and Rights

As users, you have the right to access your data, request correction, or deletion. You can also update your profile information anytime in the app.

10. Third-Party Links

External links (e.g., for discount codes) may exist in the app. PicToPlateAI is not responsible for the privacy policies of these external sites.

11. Policy Updates

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Important updates are announced within the app or on our website.

12. Contact

For all privacy questions and requests: mecosinteractive@gmail.com or hello@pictoplateai.com
